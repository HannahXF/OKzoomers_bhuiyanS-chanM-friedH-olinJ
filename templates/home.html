Information about our app.

On this website, you can collect trading cards of all of your favorite basketball players.

Trivia:

To earn new cards first go to the trivia page.
Once on the trivia page, you will be prompted with 10 multiple choice trivia questions about sports.
If you correctly answer seven or more trivia questions correctly, you will be rewarded with three new player cards.

Collecting cards:

If you answer enough trivia questions, you will be rewarded with cards.
The cards generated will be random.
Once you earn cards, you can view all of the cards you've ever earned under the inventory tab. {% endblock %}
