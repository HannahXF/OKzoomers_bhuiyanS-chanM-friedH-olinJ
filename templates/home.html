Trivia

To earn new cards you have to play our trivia game. You will be given 10 random trivia questions about a variety of different sports. If you answer at least 5 of those questions correctly, you will receive the same number of reward cards.
The reward card can be of any basketball player. Cards have the player's picture, their name, their average points per game, as well a rarity assigned to it.

Inventory

Once you win some cards, you're probably going to want to see them. When you win cards on this website, they are automatically added to your inventory. When you open up your inventory all of your cards in all its glory will show up so you can flex on your friends.


That's all you need to know to start playing!
